Formula:


an=a1+(n-1)d

Componets Breakdown

an: (The nth Term): This is the value of the number at a specific position. If you are looking for the 50th number in the list, that number is an.

a1; (The First Term): This is the starting point of your pattern. It is the very first number in the sequence.

n: (The Position): This represents "which" number you are talking about (1st, 2nd, 10th, etc.). It is always a whole number.

d: (Common Difference): This is the "rule" or the constant amount added or subtracted between each step.
