Examples:

1.) In an arithmetic sequence whose first term is 5 and the common difference between each succeeding term is -3

What are the 40th and 52th terms?

Given:
a1 (1st term) = 5
d = -3

We are looking for:
a40 (40th term) = ?
a52 (52h term) = ?

Use: An=A1+(n-1)d

a40 = 5 + (40 - 1) (-3)
a40 = 5 + (39) (-3)
a40 = 5 + (-117)
a40 = -112

a52 = 5 + (52 - 1) (-3)
a52 = 5 + (51) (-3)
a52 = 5 + (-153)
a52 = -148

2.) Suppose you have an arithmetic sequence where your 1st term is 10 and your 5th term is 34. Find the common difference.

Given:
a1 = 10
a5 = 34
n = 5

Use: An=A1+(n-1)d

34 = 10 + (5 - 1) (d)
34 = 10 + (4) (d)
34 - 10 = 4d
24 = 4d
24/4 = (4d)/4
6 = d
d = 6

3.) ?, _, _, _, _, _, _, _, _, _, _, 52. Find the 1st term with the common difference of 4.

Given:
a12 = 52
n = 12
d = 4

Use: An=A1+(n-1)d

52 = a1 + (12-1) (4)
52 = a1 + (11) (4)
52 = a1 + 44
52 - 44 = a1
a1 = 8
